# Repository Instructions

Before making unitone-specific changes, consult the relevant documents in
[`docs/maintainers/`](docs/maintainers/README.md) when they may affect the task:

- Product behavior, principles, constraints, and terminology:
  [`product-context.ja.md`](docs/maintainers/product-context.ja.md)
- Design decisions and their rationale:
  [`decisions.ja.md`](docs/maintainers/decisions.ja.md)
- Confirmed bugs, compatibility issues, and environment-specific problems:
  [`known-issues.ja.md`](docs/maintainers/known-issues.ja.md)

Consult these Japanese documents regardless of the language used in the current
conversation. Do not translate Japanese maintainer documentation into English
without a specific reason.

Before finishing each task, decide whether it produced reusable,
unitone-specific knowledge. Search the existing maintainer documents first and,
only when an update is warranted, add or revise the appropriate entry in
Japanese. Keep facts separate from hypotheses, avoid duplicate or speculative
entries, and do not record secrets or transient work state. Follow the detailed
maintenance workflow in [`docs/maintainers/README.md`](docs/maintainers/README.md).

If a proposed change conflicts with an existing recorded decision, explain why.
When maintainer documentation is updated, include a concise summary of that
update in the final report.
